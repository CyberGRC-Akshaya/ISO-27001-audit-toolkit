## 1. Audit Objectives

This internal audit is conducted to:
1. Verify the conformance of the ISMS with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements
2. Verify the conformance of the ISMS with the organisation's own policies and objectives
3. Evaluate the effectiveness of information security controls
4. Identify opportunities for improvement
5. Provide management with objective evidence for the Management Review

## 6. Audit Communication

**Escalation Protocol:**
- Significant finding requiring immediate attention → CISO notified same day
- Potential legal/regulatory issue → Legal/DPO notified within 4 hours
- Evidence of fraud or criminal activity → Audit suspended; MD/Board notified immediately

**Confidentiality:**
All audit findings are confidential to [Organization Name] management. External disclosure requires written approval from CISO.
